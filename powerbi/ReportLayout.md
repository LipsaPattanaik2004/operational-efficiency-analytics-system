# Power BI Report Layout

## Page 1 — Production Efficiency
- KPI cards: Total Units, Defect Rate, Units/Min
- Line chart: Units by day
- Bar chart: Units by shift
- Table: Machine-wise performance

## Page 2 — Downtime Analysis
- Bar chart: Downtime by reason
- Heatmap: Downtime by day & machine
- Table: Top downtime-causing machines

## Page 3 — Energy Analytics
- Trend line: kWh by day
- KPI: Avg kWh per unit
- Scatter chart: kWh vs Units Produced
